In-painting of paint losses and abrasion in a portrait

New and old flat fills were textured using a scalpel to imitate the surrounding cracked paint layers. Retouching was carried out including in-painting of the filled losses and areas of paint layer abrasion caused by past injudicious cleaning. Milky discolouration in the foreground and background passages was glazed over. Finally, the painting was spray-varnished.
