About

After her History of Art BA (Hons) at the University of Birmingham, Julia went on to achieve a Distinction at Northumbria University for the Conservation of Easel Paintings MA in 2018. During her conservation training she was awarded several scholarships, including the Anna Plowden Trust Conservation Training Bursary and the Sir Richard Stapley Educational Trust Postgraduate Bursary. As part of her training, Julia undertook a series of internships at Stichting Restauratie Atelier Limburg, Manchester Art Gallery, Guildhall Art Gallery and Tyne and Wear Museums. Since June 2019 she has been working as an Assistant Paintings Conservator at Critchlow and Kukkonen Ltd, a private conservation studio in Sheffield, UK.
