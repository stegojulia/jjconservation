Repair of deformed tears and full table lining

The paintingâ€™s main structural concern was several large tears which additionally caused deformation of the canvas. After initial consolidation, cleaning and protecting the turnover edges with facing, the support was carefully removed from the stretcher. Tear repair with welding powder and iron was then carried out, with local moisture treatment and weights used as necessary to release the deformations and flatten the canvas. Canvas inserts and fibres were added at this stage where necessary. The canvas was then reinforced with full BEVA lining carried out on a lining table and restretched.
