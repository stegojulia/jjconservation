Glue-paste lining

Preparing the secondary support for wax-resin lining

Previous Next
Traditional lining techniques workshop

In November 2016 I had an opportunity to participate in a workshop at Northumbria University which introduced the theory and practice of glue-paste and wax-resin lining. In the course of the workshop I learned about the lining process, risks, aesthetic impact on the object and the implications for later conservation treatment.
